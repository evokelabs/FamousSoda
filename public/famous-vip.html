If you have dreams of fame and fortune (let’s face it, who doesn’t?), we want to hear from you.

What if we told you that you could make money by selling Famous soda, without ever leaving your palace? True stars should have the luxury of working in comfort and style, naturally. THE ROLE.

It’s simple and the best bit is, we don’t want you to leave comfort or safety of your home.

You will be selling our sodalicious Famous Sodas that have zero sugar, are 100% natural, Australian made and only 8 calories a bottle. If you haven’t had the pleasure of tasting one of our Famous Sodas, then you don’t know what you’re missing.

UNCAPPED EARNINGS.

At the end of each month we will tally up the scores and you will be paid to your nominated bank account through your ABN.
